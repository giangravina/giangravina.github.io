JS Dice is a simple Vanilla JS game that allows users to play dice! Users are able to roll the dice until they roll a '1', once this happens it is the other player's turn. Users are also able to 'hold' the dice which keeps the player's score and also makes it the other player's turn. The first player to reach a score of 20 wins the game.
